Life in a nutshell

Hello there! My name is Nick,


I am a 3D-Artist that made a career switch into the web developers world!
A year ago, I heard about a course that would make a web developer out of you in just 7 months. Sounds crazy right?

Well, I have witnessed a lot of people growing from 0 to be able to set up an MVC system, on their own!
The organization behind this was BeCode. I believe strongly in the way that people were being trained and saw myself growing every day.

When I reached the end of the course, I got a job offering by BeCode, to become a full-time coach for new students.
I did not hesitate and took the offer, and I loved every single moment of it.

While I love to work with and help people, I think it's time to start with the real deal, and that is working for companies like yours!

Hi, My name is Nick, and I am a front-end developer with a passion for learning new things.

Hope to see you soon!
